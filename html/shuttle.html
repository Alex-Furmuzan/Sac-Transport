Shuttle Transportation

Sacramento State is committed to improving the air quality throughout our campus and that starts with the way you get here.

All students, faculty, and staff are able to help improve the air quality of the campus by utilizing Sacramento State's shuttle transportation. Paratransit, buses and shuttles, are a great option for many reasons. An average car produces nearly 23.5 lbs of CO2 to transport 1 person 30 miles while a shuttle produces around 80 lbs to transport an average of 40 to 80 people. Additionally, all students and staff who have a OneCard can use their commuter sleeve for all Sacramento Regional Transit shuttles and the Light Rail.

View down below for links for information about all available shuttle lines and visit the Online Website or the UTAPs office with your OneCard to get a commuter sleeve.

Gold and Green Lines

  • Gold Line (image): provides service to Howe Avenue, Exposition Boulevard, Heritage Lane, Arden Way, Fulton Avenue and Northrop Avenue. (Schedule Download)
  • Green Line: provides service to College Town Drive, La Riviera Drive, Folsom Boulevard, Bicentennial Way, Hornet Drive and State University Drive. (Schedule Download)

Hornet and Stinger Lines

  • Stinger Line: provides service to Napa Hall, Folsom Hall, The WELL and the 65th Street light rail station. (Schedule Download)
  • Hornet Line: provides service around the perimeter of campus, The WELL and Folsom Hall. (Schedule Download)
