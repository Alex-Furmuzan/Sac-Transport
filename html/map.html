COVID-19: Student Fees

Sacramento State is working hard to ensure that classes for full academic credit and campus operations continue safely during the pandemic, and most student services will be available remotely. Tuition is set by the CSU Board of Trustees. These links provide important information about tuition and mandatory student fees at Sacramento State:

  • Current tuition and fees, a detailed breakdown.
  • Interim CSU refund policy for tuition and fees, the system-wide framework to guide local campus decisions concerning the refund of fees charged by the campus.
  • Emergency grants from the Higher Education Emergency Relief Fund (HEERF) for students facing pandemic-related financial hardship.
