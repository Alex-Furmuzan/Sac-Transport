Welcome to Sac Transport!

Before We Get Started...

This page will guide new and incoming students through their research and journey on selecting the mode of transportation that fits them best. If you are unsure about what mode of transportation you will be choosing, we are here to help you!

To Get Started...

Navigate through the tabs below and read the information included to learn about the several transportation options you have! Take the survey at the end to finalize your decision on your mode of transportation for the coming school year!

Get Educated!

Mobile

This mode of transportation includes walking, renting a jump bike, or even scootering. There are several on campus options to not only get exercise, but contribute to saving the environment.

Shuttle

The hornet shuttle is often a forgotten mode of transportation despite its flexibility and the wide range it covers around the Sacramento State campus.

Carpool

Along with the discounted price of a parking permit, choosing to carpool as your main source of transportation will grant you with additional exclusive parking spots on campus.

Single-Occupant Vehicle

Though a popular mode of travel for students, especially those who are unaware of other options, single-occupant-vehicles are not the only mode of transportation to Sacramento State.

Maps

Find out your different transportation options visually by inputting your address into a map and observing the different modes of travel according to your location.

Survey

This survey will help Sacramento State IRT determine if this experience has been impactful to new and incoming Sacramento State students like you.
