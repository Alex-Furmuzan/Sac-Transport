Carpool

One of the easiest ways to acheive better air quality on our campus is by carpooling. By sharing a ride with someone, you benefit our campus and benefit yourself.

The act of carpooling saves millions in fuel consumption as well as greenhouse gas emissions. You get benefits for sharing a ride as well through the special Carpool permit.

More Reasons to Carpool

  • Carpooling can reduce the amount of miles traveled by a vehicle by about 23%
  • If 100 cars added another passenger, it would save an estimated 820 million gallons of gasoline and 7.2 millions of greenhouse gas emmissions every year
  • In addition to reducing fuel consumption, carpooling can result in lower overall commute costs through savings on gas and parking
  • Participating in carpooling also gives you access to HOV lanes as well as better parking spots

Carpool Parking Permit

Save on parking with the Sacramento State Carpool Parking permit. With this permit, students and staff can gain access to better parking spots during the Fall and Spring semesters. A carpool needs two members to apply and all members trying to obtain a permit have to be present at the UTAPs office at the time of purchase (Up to 4 members can apply). More than one member has to be present when parking in order to parking in preferential parking. Visit "Resources" on this page for an application.
