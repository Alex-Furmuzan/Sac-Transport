Mobile Transport

Peak Adventures' Bike Shop

  • Bikes starting at $205 with a student discount!
  • Bike maintenance available if you ever need a tuneup!
  • Classes and clinics are also available for students!

Jump Bikes

  • Don't have a bike or the funds to purchase one? No problem! Install the Lime app to get started.
  • Jump bikes provide a budget option for students who need transportation ASAP!
  • Start off by paying a small fee to unlock a local jump bike
  • From there, you pay cents for every minute you possess the bike
  • When done, simply relock the bike as you found it

Lime Scooters

  • Don't want to pedal? Rent a scooter!
  • Find the nearest scooter on the Lime app
  • After that, it's the same process as the jump bike

Why Go Mobile?

  • Get that exercise in!
  • Free parking for bikes!
  • Cut down on emissions! (Save the turtles)

Bike Map
